WORKFLOW

From URL to Insight in Four Steps

ParseSense streamlines the entire data collection process, turning complex web scraping into a simple, repeatable workflow.

1

Configure Sources

Enter URLs individually or in bulk. Choose whether to scrape just the target page or intelligently follow child links across the entire site. Set depth limits and URL patterns for precise control.

2

AI Parsing & Schema

Our AI automatically detects data structure and generates optimal schemas. Smart field mapping identifies patterns, while built-in corrections handle edge cases and ensure data quality.

3

Transform & Validate

Review and refine your structured output. Apply custom transformations, validate data quality, and preview results before delivery. Fine-tune field mappings with visual tools.

4

Deliver Anywhere

Send to databases via Airbyte connectors, export as CSV/JSON/Markdown, create embeddings for AI applications, or stream to real-time data pipelines. Your data, your destination.
